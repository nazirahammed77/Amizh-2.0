About

AMIZH Birth&Yoga

AMIZH (derived from Tamil word “Amirtham”) is founded with passionate aim of empowering the beautiful pregnant mothers with calm and confidence as she is about to begin her greatest endeavour as a ‘MOTHER’.

Pregnancy brings in a forever change in your body, your mind, your perception towards life, your prioroties and your goals.

Welcoming and accepting the change gracefuly & positively by preparing her body & mind a little ahead with right knowledge and tools can certainly make her pregnancy & motherhood journey a cherishable and joyful one

About the Founder

Dr.Krishnapriya Loganathan

Krishnapriya Loganathan, founder of AMIZH Birth&Yoga, has her Doctorate in Medical sciences from Sweden and is a Child Birth Educator (CAPPA USA), a Lactation Professional (ACLP from Begin Ed India), a Prenatal and Postnatal Yoga Teacher (RYT-200 & RPYT-100, Yoga alliance, USA).

She writes for India's top pregnancy and parenting magazine, "Gravid," on childbirth , breastfeeding and parenting . She wants her pregnant mom's to feel the pleasure of a satisfying childbirth experience like she did.   The founder believes giving birth should be Mom's proudest accomplishment, not her worst fear. At AMIZH Birth&Yoga, each mom is treated individually to fulfil their birthing and parenting dreams.
