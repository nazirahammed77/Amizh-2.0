AMIZH Birth&Yoga

AMIZH (derived from Tamil word “Amirtham”) is founded with passionate aim of empowering the beautiful pregnant mothers with calm and confidence as she is about to begin her greatest endeavour as a ‘MOTHER’.

Pregnancy brings in a forever change in your body, your mind, your perception towards life, your prioroties and your goals.

Welcoming and accepting the change gracefuly & positively by preparing her body & mind a little ahead with right knowledge and tools can certainly make her pregnancy & motherhood journey a cherishable and joyful one

"It's never too late to start"


Motivated by this realization of wanting to share and help pregnant moms have a joyful pregnancy, I conducted a survey among 150 women (Mothers) who had giving birth not more than a year ago in different cities in India, collecting their birth stories, what they wish they knew before and suggestions for future expectant mothers. The results were eye-opening. Almost all the women unanimously agreed that they would have opted for professional guidance and support if they have known was available and that proper knowledge and awareness could have transformed their pregnancy and postpartum experiences to a much more better experience than the one they had. It also became evident that every pregnancy is unique.


Following are few key aspects that the pregnant mother’s participated in survey wished someone had told before giving birth


  • Types of Birth
  • How to choose a Doctor or a hospital?
  • How to choose a Doctor or a hospital?
  • What to expect in several trimesters?
  • What to expect in labor room?
  • How does labor progress?
  • How to Breastfeed?
  • What to expect the first 24hrs after giving birth?
  • What to expect in postpartum period?
  • Whom to seek help when there is breastfeeding or postpartum issue?
  • How to recover after giving birth?
  • What about contraception options after giving birth?
